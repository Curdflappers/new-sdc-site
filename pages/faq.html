Frequently Asked Questions
Students

This club looks great! How do I join? Do I have to apply?

If you are interested in joining, you can find us at the Student Org Fair! No need to apply, anyone can join! Be sure to visit our booth and sign up for our mailing list! It's also a good opportunity to meet the executives face to face! The latest information about the Org Fair can be found here (we also give away free stickers).

I would like to join but am unable to attend the Student Org Fair. Can I still sign up?

Yep! You can join our mailing list by inputting your information here. Expect to be contacted September 15th with an update about our first meeting!.

I'm not a Computer Science major, can I still join?

Yes, however our events and curriculum are geared more towards Computer Science majors. If you are not a CS major, you are still encouraged to join the club

What kind of time commitment can I expect in joining the club?

You can expect a weekly time commitment of anywhere from 60 to 90 minutes. Occasionally longer events occur, such as our hackathon. Our mini-hackathon takes place once a semester and lasts 12 hours (10am to 10pm). While members are encouraged to attend, they are not required to. We occasionally have additional events, such as company campus tours, which are optional and last about 1-2 hours.

Wait, did you say something about a hackathon?

Yes! For our members only, of course. It's a 12-hour event that happens once a semester filled with contests, prizes, and food! We'll be releasing the full itinerary as the date gets closer; our Fall 2017 Hackathon is currently scheduled for September 23rd at 10am!

Will I have to pay any dues?

Yes, the Software Development Club collects dues to pay for food at events. Though we do have corporate sponsors, we believe our sponsors' money is better spent on resources for students. Dues are $10 per member.

I am interested in joining the executive board, how can I apply?

At the end of every semester we take applications for the board. We consider many factors: your club attendance rate, club engagement, overall demeanor, and technical experience (if you are applying for a position which requires it). We are looking for extremely motivated individuals willing to ensure the long-term success of the SDC, and it doesn't look too bad on your resumé either!
